Aya Hirano Leaves Her Agency

As many may have heard already, due to a recent scandal involving Aya Hirano and members of her band, Aya’s public image and career has been in jeopardy. Space Craft Entertainment, the agency that has managed the career of voice actress Aya Hirano, confirmed with the music news source Natalie that she is leaving the agency as of August 20 (source: AnimeNewsNetwork). The official Aya Hirano fan club, Aya Dama, will cease its operations on August 20. Aya has posted on her blog that she has already selected a new agency to represent her.
